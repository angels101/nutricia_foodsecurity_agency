# nutricia_foodsecurity_agency

## About
Nutricia Food Security Agency is a local based entity that ensures that all kinds of food are available and easy to access by the customer. It also trains the partners mostly made of customers across Africa the best utilization of available foods to score the stability of food flow in the region eliminating malnutrition. Food security is “the condition in which all people, at all times, have physical and economic access to sufficient, safe and nutritious food to meet their dietary needs and food preferences for an active and healthy life.”The four pillars of food security are availability, access, utilization, and stability.
